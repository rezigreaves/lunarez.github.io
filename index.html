About

Ann-Therese (Rezi) Greaves is a currently a student at Howard Community College studying Audio and Video Media Production and Digital Culture with an emphasis on production. Passionate about media production and visual arts, Rezi is currently a candidate for an Associate of Science in Production, and plans to complete her studies with a Bachelor’s degree in film and media arts. Born and raised in Laurel, Maryland, Rezi plans to transfer to University of Maryland Baltimore County.

An honor’s student since 9th grade, Rezi participated in the Science and Technology program at Eleanor Roosevelt High School in Greenbelt, graduating with honors and 26 college credits from Advanced Placement classes. She played with the school’s girl’s soccer team for two years, led a student-run bible study club, and participated in a month-long beautification project where she helped design and paint murals promoting environmentally-conscious themes on the school’s outdoor temporary classrooms. Interested in more than just academics, Rezi spent multiple summers volunteering with the Appalachian Service Project, tutoring elementary to high school students, and curating a library for an orphanage in Liberia. She has volunteered with her church as an acolyte, greeter, reader, youth worship service organizer, as well as leading a fundraising and recruitment campaign for the CROP Hunger Walk.

During her gap year, Rezi started developing her skills in video editing, graphic design, and social media management, and took an internship with a new podcast, A Podcast About Nothing. Rezi created video and photo assets daily to post on the podcast’s Instagram page, and was in charge of captioning, posting and tracking page growth. She also filmed and edited a behind-the-scenes video for a photoshoot for the podcast and produced, wrote, and guest-hosted episode on mental health awareness. During her time working with the podcast, she also organized and created posts to advertise paid sponsorships, discounts, and sponsored product giveaways.

Recently, Rezi has been doing freelance graphic design work for an IT consulting and business management company to create various assets the company commissioned, including business card designs, graphics for presentations, capability statements, and graphics for the company website. She has also been working with her brother to create visualizers for his music, as well as some additional freelance video editing and graphic design work projects on the side. She hopes to gain more experience in video production during her time at Howard Community College and at her four-year school.
